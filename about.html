Frequently Asked Questions

What is Arakathir’s main goal?

To guide students towards inner excellence, clarity, and positive societal contribution through structured human values and excellence education.

Who can join the program?

Any college or institution in Tamil Nadu that believes in holistic development for their students can invite Arakathir to implement the program.

What do students gain from this program?

They gain self-awareness, discipline, peace, clarity in career and relationships, and a strong foundation in life values.
